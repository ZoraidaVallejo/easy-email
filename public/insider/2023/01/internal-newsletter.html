Milestones & Accomplishments

Let’s Find Out What’s Been Achieved So Far by Our Teams at Justia

Justia Elevate. Our Justia Elevate Team continued to release new features and patches for our clients. This month, several new options for various stripes got published along with other performance optimizations, minor features, and even an upgrade to the Justia Elevate WordPress installation.

Blasts. The number of blasts and email marketing campaigns we process exploded this year! This month, we continued with our efforts to reach our contact base to promote our products and services. One of the crucial campaigns during this month was promoting Justia Elevate to attorneys in the Justia Lawyer Directory.

GA4 Support for AMP in Clients' Blogs. Continuing our migration to GA4, we have updated our client WordPress installations to support Google Analytics 4 event tracking features.

IT Maintenances. As usual, we continued to ensure the stability of our online services through ongoing server maintenance. This month, Justia projects like the Justia Lawyer Directory, Justia Law, and Justia Dockets got infrastructure updates. Even clearinghouse.net, a Justia-friend property, got a new development environment.

Justia.com SSL Certificate Renewal. The SSL certificate, a vital component of our systems, for our main domain justia.com was renewed and uploaded to several servers.

Justia Ask A Lawyer Automated QA. We continued with our efforts to increase our automated QA coverage for our properties. This month, the first version of the automated QA for Justia Ask A Lawyer was pushed live.

JUSTIA PORTAL NEWS

Take a Look at the Ongoing Development of Our Consumer Portal
  • 50-State Surveys. We concluded the updates to our 50-state alimony survey in the Divorce center. The updates elucidate how alimony can be terminated in each state.
  • Additional Snippets. We have improved the accessibility and clarity of the Small Business and Intellectual Property centers by writing more snippets highlighting critical points in these areas.
  • Disability Law Center. We completed a new center with roughly 40 pages for our Justia Legal Guides on benefits available for people with disabilities and their rights in areas such as employment, housing, and education.

Antonio Gonzalez Won 1st Place at a National-Level Technology Contest

Kava is a Mental Health Information System developed by a multidisciplinary team from the Academic Units of Psychology and Electrical Engineering at the Autonomous University of Zacatecas. Juan Antonio González, a FrontEnd Intern at Justia Mexico, is part of the forenamed team. The team recently participated in ExpoCiencias Nacional 2022 and was granted international accreditation to participate in the "Encuentro de Jóvenes Investigadores," Spain 2023.

Collaborators: Alejandra Medina, Alejandro Moreno, Arturo Jara, Erika Azuela, Haydee Garcia, Jose Ramos, Lauren Moskie, Miguel Berlanga, Rene Peralta, and Zoraida Vallejo.
