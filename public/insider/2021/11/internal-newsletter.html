Let’s Find Out What’s Been Achieved So Far by Our Teams at Justia

Justia Connect Basic Launch. Justia Connect is live. One of the most significant launches we have ever seen at Justia is finally completed. Support Page. Our team added updates for Justia Connect support. Specifically, we created a Justia Connect Frequently Asked Questions section.

Upgrades for All WordPress Installations. We upgraded all our WordPress installations to the latest version. This covers all our client blogs, Justia blogs (such as Onward and Verdict), and even Justia friends blogs (like Jurist and LawSites).

IT Maintenances. Among other IT Team maintenances, we got upgrades for our internal IT assets tool, our JIRA/Confluence Documentation server, and the backend installation for Goldman blogs which was also migrated over to VPC, highly reducing security risks.

Dates to Remember

Day of the Dead - November 1st and 2nd

The Day of the Dead is a two-day Mexican holiday when people visit the graves of deceased loved ones and set up altars with their favorite dishes, drinks, and photos. During this holiday, people also welcome back the souls of their dead relatives for a brief reunion at home that includes food and beverages.

*This is a non-federal holiday. It is not officially recognized. For more information about paid holidays, please visit Justikia.

Thanksgiving - November 25th and 26th

Thanksgiving is an annual holiday in the United States, which falls on the fourth Thursday of November. It celebrates the story of the Pilgrim's meal with the Native Americans and is reserved as a day to spend with loved ones and for giving thanks. Most families observe it with a large meal with family and friends and watch football games.
