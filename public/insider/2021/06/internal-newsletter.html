Milestones & Accomplishments

Let’s Find Out What’s Been Achieved So Far by Our Teams at Justia

Main Portal Updates. We converted four more centers to the new format and wrote step-by-step instructions on incorporating a business in six key states. We also added more than 200 snippets to the Criminal Law center. We continued to update the COVID-19 resources, including the changes to mask mandates and travel restrictions. Furthermore, we added content to the Tax center about tax benefits in Puerto Rico.

Justia Lawyer Directory. We added advice to 19 different practice areas on hiring a lawyer and updated sales proposals with a new look and feel that highlights our Lawyer Directory’s paid benefits. We also improved contact spam email blocking for administrators, added lazy loading for profile images on all listing pages, and included optimizations on our backend to reduce server loads.

Oyez Transcript Alignment Improvements. We updated one of the libraries we use to produce the oral argument feature in Oyez. It is a huge time-saver for the folks in charge of processing each new case from the Supreme Court.

Justia Amplify. This month, the team has accomplished several tasks, including rolling out the $500 Google Ads credit to existing Justia clients, transitioning PPC keywords from Broad Match Modified to Phrase Match, and implementing new conversion action labels that will help us to better track client conversions from ads.

RDS Databases Upgrades. We are continuing with the mass upgrade to our databases project. We recently completed the changes for Dockets and Blawgsearch. There are a lot more upgrades coming in the following months.

JurisPro Spam Detection Improvements. In light of the increasing spam affecting network security and performance, our team pushed an improved spam detection system for JurisPro and for our Justia friends live.

Contact Forms for Clients and Landing Pages. Behind the scenes, our team completed the first milestone for improving the processing of contact forms this month. We will now be able to add new features.

Regulations. While some regulations remain unchanged, others may evolve due to revisions, transformations, or transitions. To keep our portal up to date and engaging for our readers, our regular quarterly update of U.S. State Regulations was successfully published this month.

Justia Writing Services. Our transition from internal Writing Services to an external writer referral service will be complete. Although a small group of clients is continuing under the old system, no others will be added. Going forward, if a client asks about getting content, forward the request directly to Chris so that he can match them up with a writer.

Dates to Remember
Father's Day (Mexico and the U.S.) - June 20th

While it is not a public holiday, people in Mexico and the U.S. annually celebrate Father’s Day on the third Sunday of June. It is an observance to honor fathers and male parental figures in both countries. It is also an opportunity to express your love,

appreciation, and gratitude. On this day, people thank all those men who set solid examples and guide us throughout our lives.

The Benefits of Gardening

Believe it or not, in addition to reaping beautiful flowers and nutritious harvests for your table, gardening can do wonders for your well-being to make it through these tough times. Roll up your sleeves, start digging, planting, and weeding as a healthy alternative to cope with this "new normal." Find below a list of several of these benefits:

● Gardening burns a lot of calories and can lower your blood pressure.

● Spending time outside is good for your bones.

● Growing your own food can help you eat healthier.

● Gardening can relieve stress.

● Gardening can make you happier.

REVIEWS & RECOMMENDATIONS

Alien Worlds

Available on Netflix

Are you one of those people who's always thinking about life in outer space? Do you like animals? Do you appreciate good gaming graphics? If so, then you should watch this four-part sci-fi nature docufiction miniseries. For me, the best thing about the series is the thinking process behind it, how the creators figured out how physics and each planet's environment can impact the survival skills of living creatures. From forever-flying animals to parasitic deathly fungi, I think it is fascinating how the creators replicated Earth-like life into outer space planets.

- Miguel Lopez
