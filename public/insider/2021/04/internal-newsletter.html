Milestones & Accomplishments

Let’s Find Out What’s Been Achieved So Far by Our Teams at Justia

Justia Product Names: At a recent meeting, Tim announced that our products have evolved and received consistent new names that deliver the right message. The new product names are Justia Lawyer Directory, Justia Elevate, Justia Connect, and Justia Amplify.

Justia Onward and Avanza Blogs Ramping Up: Our team put our blogs on full throttle, upping content production to set an all-time record nine posts per blog in one month.

Social Media Efforts: Our team has revamped and unified all of our social media accounts (Twitter, Facebook, and LinkedIn) with a new design, slogan (Justia For All), and hashtag (#JustiaForAll). The effort has paid off with an increase in the number of followers and fans.

Calls Sorted by Day and Hour Added to the Justia Amplify Report: We are now bringing in more comprehensive information to our LSA clients' reports with LSA call patterns.

Justia Law Database Upgrade: This is the first of many database infrastructure upgrades that we will be performing on several Justia properties. For this first upgrade, we created a tool to automate the QA process.

Justia Internal Development Package Improvements: We have been updating the packages to share codes among our applications. It is now easier to implement new features that are compatible with all our applications and maintain consistency across the board.

Dockerized Oyez.org Transcript Converters: Justia provides Supreme Court oral argument transcripts synced with the case audio. Now, our visitors can follow each case more accurately. We also improved the process to generate this type of content by reducing both time spent and server costs.

Openjurist.org Infrastructure Upgrades: Our friends at openjurist.org can now work with an upgraded version of MySQL, courtesy of Justia.

New Labor Law Pages Added to the Justia Mexico Portal: The pages cover the labor justice system, the Federal Center of Labor Conciliation and Registry, prejudicial conciliation procedures, collective bargaining agreements, and remote work policy.

Dates to Remember

Earth Day - April 22nd

Earth Day has been celebrated annually around the globe for the past 50 years. The observance has become a global event to promote awareness to protect our environment's health, creatures, and habitats.

Children's Day - April 30th

Children's Day is an annual celebration in Mexico. On this date, most children have parties at home and school. Children are seen as a vital part of the community, so this day focuses on the importance of loving, accepting, and appreciating our children.

Stacy now serves as an Expert-in-Residence at Harvard Innovation Labs. The Experts-in-Residence (EiR) Program brings together active entrepreneurs and business leaders to advise Harvard students and help nurture their ideas. Congratulations, Stacy, on your new career endeavor!
