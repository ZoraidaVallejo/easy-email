Milestones & Accomplishments

Let’s Find Out What’s Been Achieved So Far by Our Teams at Justia

HelpDesk. We have completed a significant upgrade to the HelpDesk interface, covering both the application and infrastructure. This ensures that we are now using the latest version and benefiting from enhanced functionality.

QA Automation. This month, we have integrated additional features into our HelpDesk QA tests. Furthermore, our visual regression tests now encompass numerous Justia properties, such as the Justia U.S. Supreme Court Center, the Justia Mexico Portal, Justia Marketing, and Justia Law.

Justia Elevate. We are continually enhancing Justia Elevate. In addition to our regular updates, we have introduced new information blocks and process stripes, offering users more versatile tools. Furthermore, we prioritize security by upgrading to the latest version of WordPress.

Justia Business Contracts. We have rolled out a search bar for Justia Business Contracts. This enhancement encompasses contract categories, industries, companies, and titles, streamlining the process for all users to find the necessary information.

Justia Connect. We have implemented a convenient "Add to Calendar" feature, enabling users to add upcoming webinars to their calendars effortlessly.

Justia Jobs. We released the newest version of Justia Jobs. A new platform for our users to search for legal jobs and explore employment opportunities. The site is automatically updated from multiple sources and, in the future, Justia Connect Pro members will be able to have their jobs featured on listing pages.

Webinars. We continue promoting Justia Webinars by adding a dedicated sidebar feed that will display our upcoming events. This component is already live in the sidebar of some properties and will be appearing in the rest soon.

JUSTIA PORTAL NEWS

Take a Look at the Ongoing Development of Our Consumer Portal
  • 50-State Surveys. We extensively researched lemon laws for the Consumer Protection Law Center, power of attorney laws for the Estate Planning Legal Center, and negligence laws for the Personal Injury Center. These surveys were designed to capture variations in laws across states, providing comprehensive insights into the legal landscape.
  • FAQs for Social Media Influencers. We enhanced our current content by addressing FTC guidelines and other legal considerations for social media influencers and advertisers collaborating with them. This improvement involved creating a set of frequently asked questions (FAQs) to provide clearer insights on the topic.
  • AI Case Summaries. We streamlined the production of summaries for new decisions from state and federal appellate courts by completing the development of an AI tool capable of operating with minimal intervention.

Bruno The Frenchie

Hey there, it's Bruno The Frenchie. I'm not your average bulldog – a bit on the larger side, with a cream-colored coat, a snazzy red nose, and a unique hairy eye. Mom says I'm beautiful, and who am I to argue? I'm a seasoned explorer, always up for checking out new places, but I admit I tire out quickly.

I'm a pizza and quesadilla enthusiast, but I have to be on my best behavior health-wise. When I hit the snooze button, I snore like a bear; rumor has it, and I make funny noises while dreaming. I'm all about the hugs and making new friends. So, if you see me strutting my stuff, don't be shy – come say hi!

Somebody Somewhere

Available on Netflix

"Pluto, released in 2023, is an anime adaptation of Naoki Urasawa's work that reimagines the classic anime Astro Boy with a more adult and serious plot. It showcases a world where AI and humans coexist socially, exploring the consequences of this dynamic. The story revolves around Gesicht, a robot Europol investigator tasked with solving a series of murders in a society where AI is programmed not to harm humans. The narrative unfolds with the underlying theme of the strict directive that "Robots must not become any more human…""

— Andres Sanchez
