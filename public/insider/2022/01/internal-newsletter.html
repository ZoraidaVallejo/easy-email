WELLNESS

Houseplant Appreciation Day at Justia

Justian green thumbs (and not-so-green thumbs) had a chance to show off their greenery when we held our Houseplant Appreciation Day. Even though National Houseplant Appreciation Day is held on January 10th each year, we didn't want to pass on the opportunity of sharing pictures of our plants and how they accompany us.

National Houseplant Appreciation Day is about raising awareness of all the benefits attached to growing and keeping plants. There are many ways we can celebrate this day, and here are a few:
  • Visit a plant nursery - and bring a plant or two home with you!
  • Give a houseplant to a friend or relative.
  • Teach someone about growing and caring for plants.
  • Talk to your plants. Many people believe plants respond to this.
  • Make sure they're well watered!
