Milestones & Accomplishments

Let’s Find Out What’s Been Achieved So Far by Our Teams at Justia

First Justia Connect Webinar: On June 23rd, we held our inaugural Justia Webinar, "SEO for Law Firm Websites: Intro to SEO & Website Content," presented by Tim. With more than 100 attendants, the webinar was very informative and successful. We have already received great feedback from lawyers, and people are excited about this series's upcoming talks. As a result, we have identified an increase in sign-ups for our Justia Connect Pro service. Thank you to all the people involved in the event. Go Justia!

Justia Connect Webinar Marketing: One of the biggest benefits of being a Justia Connect Pro Member is the Webinars. For the inaugural Justia Connect Webinar, which was open to all, we touched on various promotions through multiple campaigns, different ads, and numerous announcements on Justia properties, client reports, and client support center, among others.

Justia Elevate Sites: We hit the first big milestone after the launch this month, and we pushed the first-ever Justia Elevate platform website into production!

Mexican Constitution: We have finally started updating the content of our Mexico portal, starting with the Mexican Constitution, and improved the look and feel. This is just the first of many updates to our Mexico portal.

QA Automation: Our QA Team has been advancing our quality assurance coverage with updates to Justia Elevate, Justia Amplify, SEO Reports, SEC Contracts, and other properties.

IT Maintenance: As usual, the IT Team is working hard on maintaining several servers across the board. This affected the backend infrastructure of products such as our Justia Lawyer Directory, Free Law, and other major properties.

JUSTIA PORTAL NEWS

Take a Look at the Ongoing Development of Our Consumer Portal
  • Additional Snippets. We have improved the accessibility and clarity of the Elder Law and Social Security and Retirement Planning legal guides by writing more snippets highlighting key points in these areas.
  • Employment Law 50-State Surveys. Our most recent major project involves writing surveys of state laws on employment-related topics, such as discrimination, meal and rest breaks, and leaves. Our team will post the surveys on the Employment Law legal guide.
  • Justia Portal Design Updates. We are resuming the discussion about options for improving the Justia Supreme Court site's home page and case overview pages with our Design Team. Furthermore, we are adding a section to the sidebar of each legal guide to list related legal guides so that users can navigate more efficiently.
  • Justia Onward Blog Posts Highlighting Justia Legal Guides. We wrote a couple of blog posts on our Justia Onward blog explaining how consumers could use our Education Law and Civil Rights legal guides.

Fernando Mendoza’s Article Published in a Research Journal

Our colleague Alan Fernando Mendoza and his classmates from Computer Systems Engineering at the Technical Institute of Saltillo published an original article, “Detection of Lung Diseases Through Artificial Intelligence and the Internet of Things,” in the World Journal of Engineering Research and Technology (WJERT). WJERT is an international peer-reviewed, online bimonthly research journal for the enhancement of research in various disciplines of Engineering and Technology. Congratulations Fernando! Read the official announcement on the Facebook fan page of the Department of Computer Systems Engineering.
