JUSTIA EMPLOYEE SPOTLIGHT

Miriam Valdes Retains Her Distinction as National Researcher Level 1 at SNI

Congratulations to Miriam Valdes on keeping her distinction as a National Researcher in the Research National System (SNI, Sistema Nacional de Investigadores). In early January, CONACyT (National Council of Science and Technology) published the 2021 SNI results. From 7,753 researchers who applied to keep their distinction, only 5,810 succeeded. There is a total historical number of 36,714 national researchers. Find out more in the official press release here. Congratulations Miriam!

Dates to Remember

Mexican Constitution Day
February 5th

Constitution Day, a federal holiday in Mexico, takes place on the first Monday of February regardless of the date. On this day, people in Mexico commemorate the anniversary of Mexico’s current constitution, which was promulgated on February 5th, 1917.

Valentines Day
February 14th*

Valentine's Day, or the Feast of Saint Valentine, is celebrated on February 14th. It is a popular festival of romanticism throughout the USA and other places worldwide. On this day, people usually express their affection with greetings and gifts.

*This is a non-federal holiday. It is not officially recognized. For more information about paid holidays please visit Justikia.

REVIEWS & RECOMMENDATIONS

14 Peaks: Nothing Is Impossible

Available on Netflix

This documentary follows Nepalese climber Nirmal Purja, known as Nimsdai, on his attempt to summit all 14 of the world’s 8,000-meter mountains in just seven months, breaking the previous record of 7 years. This climbing endeavor, which he called “Project Possible,” shows Nimsdai pushing the limits of human endurance as he and his team traverse Mount Everest, K2, and 12 other iconic peaks through extreme weather.

The footage is stunning, and his ambition, perseverance, and dedication are awe-inspiring. Watch what he makes possible with a powerful positive attitude!

- Lauren Moskie
