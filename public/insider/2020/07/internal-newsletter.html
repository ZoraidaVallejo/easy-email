Milestones & Accomplishments

Let’s Find Out What’s Been Achieved So Far by Our Teams at Justia

First PPC Monthly Report Sent

After several months of development, we automated and delivered the first report for our PPC clients. This report will be sent to PPC clients in the same way we send Web and Blog clients reports each month. Thank you to everybody who was involved in making it happen!

Avanza’s First 25 Blog Posts

Since its launch on April 23rd of this year, our Spanish blog Avanza now has its first 25 blog posts published. We will continue including the most popular and recent posts from Onward for all of the Spanish-speaking community.

Automated QA for the Lawyer Directory

We now have a tool that automatically scans our directory to detect and report errors and data inconsistencies. This is crucial to offer a safe and reliable product for our clients and free up time for our team.

Reminders

Backups. Remember to back up your laptop regularly. If your external hard drive is not working, please report it immediately to your supervisor and IT department.

Video Conferences. Join meetings on time. If possible, join meetings earlier than two minutes before the start time. Do not forget to turn on the camera.
