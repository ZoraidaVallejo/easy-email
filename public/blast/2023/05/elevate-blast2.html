Are you frustrated with your website's search engine ranking? Do you find yourself being outranked by new websites or newer firms? This common problem has a solution.

3 Tips for Reclaiming Your Place

Optimize Your Page Titles

Include your target keywords on all your important pages. Page titles are important signals that help Google understand what your pages are about and their target optimization.

Potential Issues if Your Pages Lack Your Target Keywords

  • Your website may not show when clients use those location in searches

  • Your site will compete with law firm websites from other states

  • Lower quality traffic due to visitors from locations you don’t serve

  • Pages not showing for their related practice area

  • You may attract visitors not necessarily looking for legal services

What You or Your Website Provider Should Check on Your Page Titles

  • Include your main target location

  • Include the main practice area relevant foreach page

  • Include the word “lawyer” or “attorney”

  • Include the name of the page
