Tech News

Microsoft Buys LinkedIn for $26.2 Billion, Reasserting Its Muscle

On June 27, Microsoft announced that it would be acquiring the business networking company LinkedIn for $26.2 billion cash. This is Microsoft's largest acquisition ever, and its leadership has said it plans to take a more hands-off approach than it has with other recent acquisitions, including Skype and Yammer. This acquisition underscores Microsoft's shift in focus from traditional software to cloud computing.

Google ignores keywords in the TLD part of your domain name for ranking purposes

Despite rumors to the contrary, Google has confirmed that keyword-rich TLDs (e.g., myfirm.lawyer, myfirm.law, and myfirm.attorney) have no effect on rankings.

Google will factor in page speed for mobile sites in its rankings

Although the speed of your mobile pages doesn't currently affect your rankings, Google says that it may soon be a factor.

Google’s Gary Illyes talks RankBrain, Penguin update, Authorship & more

Google Webmaster Trends Analyst Gary Illyes answers some common questions about SEO trends, covering topics such as the search engine’s new RankBrain algorithm and the older Penguin algorithm. Illyes also advises watching closely for changes around accelerated mobile pages (AMPs) and chat bots.
