Tip of the Month

Set Up a Short Name on Google My Business!

There’s a new feature for verified businesses that allows you to assign a short name and URL to your Google My Business listing to make it easier for customers to find you. Please note that the short name can be 32 characters long. You can remove or edit a custom URL up to 3 times per year. Keep in mind that the short name should be associated with your business name. We recommend including your location for that particular listing.

Did You Know?

Google is Suspending Many GMB Listings

Google, after some negative media attention, has recently removed a massive number of virtual offices from Google My Business and Google Maps. In the process, Google suspended a number of listings with real addresses as well. Google is aware of this issue, but stated that it currently has a backlog of 2-3 weeks in addressing reconsideration requests (normally a reconsideration request takes 2 days).

While your listing may not have been suspended, we recommend you do NOT make any changes to your Google My Business listing for at least the next few weeks. Any change on a listing could get it suspended, and it could remain suspended until the Google support backlog subsides. If you need to make an emergency update that you think is absolutely necessary, please contact us first.
